Exercise 2. Speed of Sound Wave in Water.

Find the speed of a mechanical wave in water of density 1 g/cc and bulk modulus \(2\times 10^9\, \text{N/m}^2\text{.}\)
Answer.
\(1414\,\text{m/s}\text{.}\)
Solution.
From wave traveling in a fluid medium, we have
\begin{equation*} v = \sqrt{B/\rho} = \sqrt{\frac{2\times 10^9\ \text{N/m}^2}{1000\ \text{kg/m}^3}} = 1,414\ \text{m/s}. \end{equation*}
