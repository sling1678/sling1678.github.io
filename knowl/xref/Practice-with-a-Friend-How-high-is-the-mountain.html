Exercise 3. Practice with a Friend: How high is the mountain?

The angles subtended by the direct sight to the mountain top from home and anothe location a distanc \(D\) closer to the mountain are \(\theta_1\) and \(\theta_2\text{,}\) respectively, as shown in Figure 4.15. Find the height of the mountain.
Answer check: Your answer will be in terms of \(D,\, \theta_1, \,\theta_2\text{.}\) For \(D=5.0\text{ km}\text{,}\) \(\theta_1=10^\circ\text{,}\) and \(\theta_2=15^\circ\text{,}\) \(H=2.6\text{ km}\text{.}\)
