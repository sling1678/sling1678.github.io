Exercise 1. Practice with the Right Hand Rule.

Vector \(\vec A \) is pointed towards East and vector \(\vec B \) is pointed towards North. (a) Which way is the cross product \(\vec A \times \vec B \) pointed? (b) Which way is the cross product \(\vec B \times \vec A \) pointed?
Hint.
Use RHR.
Answer.
(a) UP, (b) DOWN
Solution.
(a) Using right-hand rule, gives the answer "UP" for the direction of \(\vec A \times \vec B \text{.}\)
(b) Using right-hand rule, gives the answer "DOWN" for the direction of \(\vec B\times \vec A \text{.}\)
