Exercise 2. Angular Magnification for Convex Lens if Eye Very Close.

What will be the formula for the angular magnification of a convex lens if the eye is very close to the lens and the near point is located a distance \(D\) from the eye?
Solution.
We just have to replace \(25\,\text{cm}\) by \(D\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} M = 1 + \dfrac{D}{f}. \end{equation*}
