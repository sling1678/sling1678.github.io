Example 41.11. Inductive Reactances.

Find reactances of the following inductors in AC circuits with the given frequencies in each case.
  1. \(2\text{-mH}\) inductor with a \(60\text{-Hz}\) frequency of the AC circuit.
  2. \(2\text{-mH}\) inductor with a \(600\text{-Hz}\) frequency of the AC circuit.
  3. \(20\text{-mH}\) inductor with a \(6\text{-Hz}\) frequency of the AC circuit.
  4. \(20\text{-mH}\) inductor with a \(60\text{-Hz}\) frequency of the AC circuit.
Answer.
(a) \(0.754\,\Omega \text{,}\) (b) \(7.54\:\Omega\text{,}\) (c) \(0.754\: \Omega\text{,}\) (d) \(7.54\:\Omega\text{,}\)
Solution.
  1. \begin{equation*} X_L = 2\pi f L = 2\pi\times 60\:\textrm{Hz}\times 2\:\textrm{mH} = 754\:\textrm{m}\Omega, \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} X_L = 2\pi f L = 2\pi\times 600\:\textrm{Hz}\times 2\:\textrm{mH} = 7.54\:\Omega, \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} X_L = 2\pi f L = 2\pi\times 6\:\textrm{Hz}\times 20\:\textrm{mH} = 754\:\textrm{m}\Omega, \end{equation*}
  4. \begin{equation*} X_L = 2\pi f L = 2\pi\times 60\:\textrm{Hz}\times 20\:\textrm{mH} = 7.54\:\Omega. \end{equation*}
