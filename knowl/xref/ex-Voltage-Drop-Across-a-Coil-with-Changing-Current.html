Exercise 4. (Calculus) Voltage Drop Across a Coil with Changing Current.

A large coil of inductance \(200\text{ H}\) is connected to a varying current source such that current passing through the coil is given by \(I = 120\text{ A }\cos(400 t)\text{.}\) Find the voltage across the inductor.
Hint.
Voltage reading is equal to the EMF induced.
Answer.
\(9.6\times 10^6\sin(400\:t) \text{ V}\text{.}\)
Solution.
The voltage across an inductor is equal to the EMF induced in the coil when current is varying with time.
\begin{align*} V \amp = L\dfrac{dI}{dt}\\ \amp = -200\times 120\times 400\times \sin(400\:t) = 9.6\times 10^6\:\sin(400\:t)\:\text{V}. \end{align*}
