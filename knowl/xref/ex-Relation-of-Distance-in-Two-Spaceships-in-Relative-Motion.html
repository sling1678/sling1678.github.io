Checkpoint 49.31. Relation of Distance in Two Spaceships in Relative Motion.

Solution.
We use length contraction to answer this.
\begin{equation*} L_B = \frac{L_A}{\gamma} = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\times 1\text{ m} = 0.866\text{ m}. \end{equation*}
