Exercise 2. Speed of Mechanical Wave in Air.

Find the speed of a mechanical wave in air of density \(1.2\, \text{kg/m}^3\) and bulk modulus \(10^5\,\text{N/m}^2\text{.}\)
Answer.
\(289\, \text{m/s}\text{.}\)
Solution.
From wave speed for sound wave in air we get
\begin{equation*} v = \sqrt{B/\rho} = \sqrt{\frac{10^5\ \text{N/m}^2}{1.2\ \text{kg/m}^3}} = 290\ \text{m/s}. \end{equation*}
