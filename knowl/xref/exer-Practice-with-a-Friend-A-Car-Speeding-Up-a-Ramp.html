Exercise 1. Practice with a Friend: A Car Speeding Up a Ramp.

To merge into a highway traffic, a car accelerates on a ramp of length \(100\text{ m}\text{.}\) Starting from rest, the car makes it all the way to the end of the ramp in \(5.4\text{ sec}\text{.}\) Asuming the acceleration was constant, (a) what is the acceleration of the car? (b) what would be the speed of the car at the end of the ramp?
Answer.
(a) \(6.86\,\text{m/s}^2\text{,}\) (b) \(37\,\text{m/s}\text{.}\)
