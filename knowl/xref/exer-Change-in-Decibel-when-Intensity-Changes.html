Exercise 2. Change in Decibel when Intensity of a Wave Changes.

The intensity of sound drops to \(10\%\) of its original intensity. What is the change in dB?
Solution.
We us the final formula from the last question to obtain.
\begin{align*} I_2 (\text{dB}) - I_1 (\text{dB}) \amp = 10 \log_{10}\left( \frac{I_2}{I_1}\right) \\ \amp = 10 \log_{10}\left( \frac{1}{10}\right) = - 10 \log_{10}(10) = -10.0 \end{align*}
