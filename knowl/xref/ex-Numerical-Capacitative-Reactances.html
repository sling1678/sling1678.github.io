Example 41.12. Capacitative Reactances.

Find reactances of the following capacitors in AC circuits with the given frequencies in each case.
  1. \(2\text{-mF}\) capacitor with a \(60\text{-Hz}\) frequency of the AC circuit.
  2. \(2\text{-mF}\) capacitor with a \(600\text{-Hz}\) frequency of the AC circuit.
  3. \(20\text{-mF}\) capacitor with a \(6\text{-Hz}\) frequency of the AC circuit.
  4. \(20\text{-mF}\) capacitor with a \(60\text{-Hz}\) frequency of the AC circuit.
Answer.
(a) \(1.33\,\Omega \text{,}\) (b) \(133\:\text{m}\Omega\text{,}\) (c) \(1.33\:\Omega\text{,}\) (d) \(133\:\text{m}\Omega\text{,}\)
Solution.
  1. \begin{equation*} X_C = \dfrac{1}{2\pi f C} = \dfrac{1}{2\pi\times 60\:\text{Hz}\times 2\:\text{mF}} = 1.33\:\Omega. \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} X_C = \dfrac{1}{2\pi f C} = \dfrac{1}{2\pi\times 600\:\text{Hz}\times 2\:\text{mF}} = 133\:\text{m}\Omega. \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} X_C = \dfrac{1}{2\pi f C} = \dfrac{1}{2\pi\times 6\:\text{Hz}\times 20\:\text{mF}} = 1.33\:\Omega. \end{equation*}
  4. \begin{equation*} X_C = \dfrac{1}{2\pi f C} = \dfrac{1}{2\pi\times 60\:\text{Hz}\times 20\:\text{mF}} = 133\:\text{m}\Omega. \end{equation*}
