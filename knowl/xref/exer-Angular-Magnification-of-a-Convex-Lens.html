Exercise 1. Angular Magnification of a Convex Lens.

What will be the angular magnification of a convex lens with the focal length \(2.5\, \text{cm}\text{?}\)
Answer.
\(11\text{.}\)
Solution.
The angular magnification is given by
\begin{equation*} M = 1 + \dfrac{25\:\textrm{cm}}{f\:\textrm{in cm}} = 1 + \dfrac{25\:\textrm{cm}}{2.5\:\textrm{cm}} = 11. \end{equation*}
