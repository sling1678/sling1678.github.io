Exercise 1. Wavelength of a Radio Wave.

A radio wave (which is an EM wave) has a frequency of \(12\, \text{MHz}\text{.}\) What is its wavelength in vacuum?
Hint.
Definition.
Answer.
\(25\, \text{m}\text{.}\)
Solution.
Since speed of the wave is \(c = 3\times 10^{8}\:\textrm{m/s}\text{,}\) we will get
\begin{equation*} \lambda = \dfrac{c}{f} = \dfrac{3\times 10^{8}\:\textrm{m/s}}{12\times 10^{6}\:\textrm{Hz}} = 25\:\textrm{m}. \end{equation*}
