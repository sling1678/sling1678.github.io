Exercise 1. Wave Speed on a Tense Rope.

Find the speed of a mechanical wave in a rope of tension \(100\,\text{N}\) and mass density \(20\, \text{g/cm}\text{.}\)
Answer.
\(7.07\, \text{m/s}\text{.}\)
Solution.
From the formula for wave speed on a taut string we have
\begin{equation*} v = \sqrt{T/\mu} = \sqrt{\frac{100\ \text{N}}{2.0\ \text{kg/m}}} = 7.1\ \text{m/s}. \end{equation*}
