Example 27.16. Number of Excited Modes in a Water Molecule.

Consider a water vapor at some temperature, where only rotation and translation modes of its molecule are excited. What is the number of excited modes?
Answer.
\(6\text{.}\)
Solution.
he molecues of water are not linear. Therefore, there will be three rotational modes. With three translational modes, the number of excited modes at the temperatures of the problem will be \(6\text{.}\)
