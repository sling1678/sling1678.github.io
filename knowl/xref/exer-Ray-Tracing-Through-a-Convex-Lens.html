Solution 2. b
You can use plane geometry or analytically geometry to solve this problem. I will illustrate the analytical geometry here. To solve by plane geometry look for similar triangles in the figure. For analytical procedure we will use the \(x\) and \(y\) axes as shown and denote the heights of the object and the image by their \(y\)-coordinates. Now, we can equate the slope of the line POQ by using the coordinates of P and O and the coordinates of O and Q. This gives
\begin{equation*} -\dfrac{y_0}{p} = \dfrac{y_i}{q},\ \ \Longrightarrow\ \ \dfrac{y_i}{y_0} = - \dfrac{q}{p}. \end{equation*}
