Example 45.29. Magnification of a Telescope from Powers of the Objective and the Eyepiece.

A telescope has an objective of focal length equal to \(1100\text{ mm}\text{.}\) When you use an eyepiece that has focal length \(20\text{ mm}\text{,}\) what will be the magnification?
Answer.
\(55\text{.}\)
Solution.
Answer is obtained simply by taking the ratio of the foci. We ignore the sign here.
\begin{equation*} |M| = \frac{f_\text{o}}{f_\text{e}} = \frac{1100}{20} = 55. \end{equation*}
