Exercise 2. Practice with a Friend: A Skier Racing Down a Hill.

A skier is racing down a hill at a constant acceleration. Assume straightline motion. She covers a distance of \(100\,\text{m}\) in \(5\text{ s}\text{.}\) At the end she is going at speed \(30\text{ m/s}\text{.}\) (a) What was her acceleration? (b) What was her speed at the beginning?
Answer.
(a) \(4\,\text{m/s}^2\text{,}\) (b) \(10\,\text{m/s}\text{.}\)
