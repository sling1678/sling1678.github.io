Exercise 3. Light Bending from a Bulb Immersed in Water.

A light bulb is immersed inside a water tank. A ray of light from the bulb is incident on the water/air interface at an angle of \(65^\circ\) from the horizontal interface. At what angle from the normal does the ray come out in the air?
Hint.
Use Snellâ€™s law.
Answer.
\(34.3^\circ\) from normal.
Solution.
\begin{align*} \theta_1 \amp = 90^{\circ}-65^{\circ} = 25^{\circ}.\\ \theta_2 \amp = \sin^{-1}\left( \dfrac{n_1\sin\theta_1}{n_2}\right)\\ \amp = \sin^{-1}\left( \dfrac{\frac{4}{3}\sin25^{\circ}}{1}\right) = 34.3^{\circ}. \end{align*}
