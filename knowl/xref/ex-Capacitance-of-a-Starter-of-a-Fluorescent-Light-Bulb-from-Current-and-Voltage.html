Exercise 1. Capacitance of a Starter of a Fluorescent Light Bulb from Current and Voltage.

A starter for a fluorescent light bulb is a capacitor. When an AC voltage \(170\; \text{V}\; \cos(2\pi\times 60 \times t)\) is applied across the capacitor, a peak current of \(1.2\,\text{A}\) flows in the capacitor. What is the capacitance?
Hint.
Use \(I_0 = V_0/|Z|\text{.}\)
Answer.
\(18.7\:\mu\text{F}\text{.}\)
Solution.
Using \(I_0 = V_0/|Z| = V_0\omega C\text{,}\) we obtain
\begin{align*} C \amp = \dfrac{I_0}{2\pi\:f\:V_0} \\ \amp = \dfrac{1.2\:\text{A}}{2\pi\:60\:\text{Hz}\times 170\: \text{V}} = 18.7\:\mu\text{F}. \end{align*}
