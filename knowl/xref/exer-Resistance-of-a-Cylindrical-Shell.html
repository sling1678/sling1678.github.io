Exercise 1. Resistance of a Cylindrical Shell.

A cylindrical shell made of a material of resistivity \(\rho\) has inner radius \(a\text{,}\) outer radius \(b\) and length \(L\text{.}\) Find its resistance for flow of current along its length.
Solution.
Use the formula for resistance of a wire.
\begin{equation*} R = \rho\dfrac{L}{A} = \dfrac{\rho\:L}{\pi\left( b^2 - a^2\right)}. \end{equation*}
