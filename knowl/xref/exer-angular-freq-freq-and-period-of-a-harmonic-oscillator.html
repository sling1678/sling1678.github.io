Exercise 1. Angular Frquesncy, Frequency, and Period of a GHarmonic OScillator.

A harmonic oscillator of mass \(200\,\text{grams}\) is attached to a spring of spring constant \(100\, \text{N/m}\text{.}\) Find (a) angular frequency, (b) frequency and (c) time period.
Answer.
(a) \(22.4\, \text{rad/sec}\text{,}\) (b) \(3.56\, \text{Hz}\text{,}\) (c) \(0.28\,\text{sec}\text{.}\)
Solution.
\begin{align*} \amp \textrm{(a) Angular frequency:}\ \ \omega = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}} = \sqrt{\frac{100\ \textrm{N/m}}{0.200\ \textrm{kg}}} = 22.4\ \textrm{rad/sec}.\\ \amp \textrm{(b) Frequency:}\ \ f = \frac{\omega}{2\pi} =\frac{22.4\ \textrm{rad/s}}{2\pi} = 3.56\ \textrm{Hz}.\\ \amp \textrm{(c) Time Periond:}\ \ T = \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{3.56\ \textrm{Hz}} = 0.28\ \textrm{s}.\\ \end{align*}
