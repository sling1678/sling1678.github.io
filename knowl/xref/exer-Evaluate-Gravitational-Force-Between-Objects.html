Exercise 1. Evaluate Gravitational Force Between Objects.

Evaluate the magnitude of gravitational force between (a) two 5-kg spherical steel balls separated by a center-to-center distance of 15 cm, (b) the Sun and the planet Neptune which have an average separation of 30.15 AU. \(M_{\textrm{Sun}} \approx 2.0\times 10^{30}\ \textrm{kg}\) and \(M_{\textrm{Neptune}} \approx 1.0\times 10^{26}\ \textrm{kg}\text{.}\)
Solution.
I will express all quantities in the SI units before putting in the formulas and leave out the units in the calculation.
\begin{equation*} \textrm{(a)}\ F = G_N \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2} = 6.67\times 10^{-11}\times \frac{5\times 5}{0.15^2} = 7.4\times 10^{-8} \ \textrm{N}. \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \textrm{(b)}\ F = 6.67\times 10^{-11}\times \frac{2.0\times 10^{30}\times 1.0\times 10^{26}}{(30.15\times1.5 \times 10^{11}) ^2} = 6.5\times 10^{20}\ \textrm{N}. \end{equation*}
