Exercise 1. Angular Magnification of a Magnifying Glass.

Find the angular magnification of an image by a magnifying glass of \(f = 5.0\, \text{cm}\) if the object is placed \(p = 4.0\, \text{cm}\) from the lens and the lens is close to the eye.
Solution.
By using the formula for \(M\) we get
\begin{equation*} M = 1 + \dfrac{25\:\textrm{cm}}{5\:\textrm{cm}} = 6. \end{equation*}
