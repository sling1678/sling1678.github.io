Exercise 2. Converting Temperature from Fahrenheit to Celsius and Kelvin.

The temperature on a nice day in Missouri, USA is \(65^{\circ}\text{F}\text{.}\) What will be the readings in the Celsius and Kelvin scales?
Hint.
Use conversion formulas.
Answer.
\(18.3^{\circ}\text{C}\text{,}\) \(291.45\text{K}\text{.}\)
Solution.
\begin{align*} C \amp = \dfrac{5}{9}(F - 32) = \dfrac{5}{9}(65 - 32) = 18.3^{\circ}.\\ K \amp = C + 273.15 = 18.3 + 273.15 = 291.45^{\circ}. \end{align*}
