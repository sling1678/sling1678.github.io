In 1920’s, Albert Michelson in the United States conducted experiments measuring the speed of light using a rotating prism as shown in Figure 43.8. Michelson set his apparatus at Mount Wilson Observatory in Southern California and the reflecting mirror on the Lookout Mountain of Mount San Antonio, about \(22\text{ miles}\) away. Careful measurements of Michelson led to a more precise value of the speed of light to be
\begin{equation*} v = 299,796\pm4\text{ km/s}. \end{equation*}
