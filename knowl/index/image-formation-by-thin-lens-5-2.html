Image by a concave lens is always virtual. That means you can look at the image when you view and object through the lens, but you cannot place the object on any screen. Ray tracing works same way here as it does for the virtual image in a cnvex lens shown in Figure 44.53. As shown in Figure 44.54, we draw two rays from a point on the object to locate its image by extension of the transmitted rays.
