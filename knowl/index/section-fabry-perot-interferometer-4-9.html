The peaks are called resonant frequencies of the cavity. The width of a peak is defined by the range of frequencies when transmittance is \(0.5\text{.}\) This width is called full width at half maximum (FWHM). Between successive peaks, say between \(f_m\) and \(f_{m+1}\text{,}\) there is low signal. This range is call free spectral range.
