Wave/Physical Optics: However, if \(\lambda \ge L\) or \(L\) only a few times \(\lambda\) so that we cannot claim \(\lambda \lt \lt \text{,}\) then ray picture will give us misleading answers. In this situation, we must use the wave picture and treat light as electromagnetic wave. Also, if we are interested in how much energy in the light ends up in the reflected part and how much in the transmitted part, we must use the wave picture. Physical optics is another term for wave optics.
