Imagine an electromagnetic wave incident on a metal. The electrons of the surface atoms will be accelerated by the electric field in the wave. In the case of the electromagnetic wave with electric field pointed towards positive \(y\) axis, electrons will accelerate in the negative \(y\) direction. At that instant, magnetic field of EM wave would be in the positive \(z\)-axis. This will result in a magnetic force on the electrons in the direction of the positive \(x\)-axis, which is the direction of the electromagnetic wave. Thus, the metal will experience a net force in the direction of the electromagnetic wave.
