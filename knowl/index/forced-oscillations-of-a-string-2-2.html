A better way to excite a string in a normal mode oscillation is to use resonance and drive the string into the normal mode. In the case of a simple harmonic oscillator, we had only one frequency to excite. Here, we have a large number of normal frequencies, and we can excite any one of them by driving at the frequency of that normal mode.
