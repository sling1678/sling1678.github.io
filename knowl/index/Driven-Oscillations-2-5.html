Notice that the denominator on the right side of Eq. (40.30) acts as the effective resistance of the series RLC circuit. This effective reistance is called impedance of the circuit. We denote impedance by capital letter \(Z\text{.}\)
\begin{equation} Z = \sqrt{R^2+\left(\omega L - \dfrac{1}{\omega C} \right)^2}.\tag{40.32} \end{equation}
