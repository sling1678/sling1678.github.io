From Eq.(34.8) the unit of resistivity in SI system will be
\begin{equation*} \frac{J}{E} = \frac{\text{C}/\text{s.m}^2}{\text{N/C}}. \end{equation*}
This is a complicated combination of units. A more common way to represent it is to use unit of resistance, which is denoted by \(\Omega\text{,}\) called Ohm. In terms of \(\Omega\text{,}\) the unit is
\begin{equation*} \text{SI unit of }\sigma = \Omega\;\text{m}. \end{equation*}
