If an object is withing a focal length of a convex lens, no image will form on any screen on the other side of the lens like we saw in Figure 44.52. When rays from the object emergy from the lens, they diverge as shown in Figure 44.53. When you are looking through the lens, the rays that started from P will appear to to come from point Q. This is virtual image of point P.
